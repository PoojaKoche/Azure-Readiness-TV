About Us

The Intelligent Cloud Readiness team has launched Azure Readiness Worldwide TV, a destination with four new “snackable” video channels: Data & AA/BI, Apps & Infrastructure, Artificial Intelligence, and IoT. These channels feature the latest videos offering high value content for sellers from a variety of sources, including Channel 9, YouTube, and others. The platform works on PCs and mobile devices, including iOS devices. The newest videos for the channel are offered in a compact, easy to consume microlearning format for Microsoft and partner use – enabling them to find, learn, and use vital information faster and more effectively. Going forward, the emphasis will be to create short videos of 10 minutes or less based on priority questions from the field.
Uniquely, Azure Worldwide Readiness TV is enabled with AI-powered search that enables viewers to find and go to the exact place in a video where a topic they are interested in is discussed.
“Often, it’s difficult for anyone in the field to take hour or days away from their job to learn new things, leading to times when perhaps they are not as prepared as they could be when engaging a customer or prospect,” said Sanjay Soni, technical readiness lead in the Intelligent Cloud Readiness team. “These new ‘snackable’ videos provide an answer – a way to learn quickly, on the go, and in time.” New videos will be created and uploaded regularly.
